Description

Sick and tired of all the other bar recipe apps that look dull?


Want to impress your first date with your "bartending skills" but don't know what you're doing?


Want to give a guy and his very first app the thumbs up?


Then you've come to the right place!


The Portable Bartender Features:


- Over 600+ drink and cocktail recipes!

- A beautiful and easy to use look and design

- A favourites system that'll keep you're most loved drinks everywhere you go

- Much much more that I can't remember off the top of my head!
